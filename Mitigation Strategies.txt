Mitigation Strategies:
1. Upgrade Software: Keep software up to date to patch vulnerabilities.
2. Configuration Hardening: Strengthen settings on servers to minimize attack surfaces.
3. Security Best Practice:  Use firewalls, intrusion detection, and secure coding methods.
4. Vulnerability Scanning: Conduct regular assessments to find and fix new vulnerabilities.
5. User Education: Train users on strong passwords and how to spot phishing attempts.
